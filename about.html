Our Mission

Hi! This is the K.A.P.S. team! Thanks for playing Striver! Currently we only have the demo available, although we hope to develop the full version in the future. Our team decided to create this game loosely based off of personal experiences in our lives and also the people in them. This game was designed to teach people that not everyone is the same. Every person can decide who they want to be.
"Every single American; gay, straight, lesbian, bisexual, transgender ; every single American deserves to be treated equally in the eyes of the law and in the eyes of our society. It’s a pretty simple proposition.”- Barak Obama
Our goal is to let people know that just because someone is different, deifferent is not always a bad thing. We hope at the end of the day, you can at least open your mind.
